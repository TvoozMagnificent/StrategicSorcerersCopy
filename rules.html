Rules of Strategic Sorcerers

Start a New Game

1. The standard game of chess with a twist from our favourite fiction series Harry Potter

2. White chooses any one piece from his army (first clicked piece) and it becomes its horcrux after which black does the same. (The horcrux cannot be the king)

3. The game is played as a normal chess game except for the fact that the pawn initially moves towards its own home rank.

4. When is hits an end rank it reverses its direction and continues on.

5. It captures a piece in the same way it moves, i.e, the piece infront it.

6. There is no enpassant or pawn promotion or castling.

7. Until the horcrux is captured the king has normal moving ability. After the horcrux has been captured the king is paralysed i.e., it cannot move.
